​Unscrew the Canteen lock covering the setter button which engages the bezel to adjust the movement.

​Press down on the setter button half way.

You can now rotate the bezel to start adjusting the date display.

​As you rotate the bezel you will see the date disc advance.

Press down on the setter button down further

You can now rotate the bezel to adjust the hour and minute hand

​to set the time.

You can adjust the time and date by first setting the date to the day before the desired date and then advance the hands to the correct time and date.

Once completed, press the setter lock button which when pressed down locks disengages the bezel from adjusting the time. 

Screw the canteen cover back on the watch.
